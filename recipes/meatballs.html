Spaghetti and Meatballs

Description

Indulge in the timeless comfort of a classic Italian favorite with our hearty spaghetti and meatballs. This beloved dish combines al dente spaghetti noodles smothered in rich, savory marinara sauce, adorned with tender, flavorful meatballs that melt in your mouth with each forkful. The aroma of garlic, herbs, and simmering tomatoes fills the air, evoking memories of family gatherings and cozy Sunday dinners. Topped with a generous sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves, every twirl of spaghetti delivers a comforting embrace that warms the soul and satisfies the senses.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Bread crumbs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Sauce
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Mix Ingredients
  3. Put in oven
  4. Wait for a bit
  5. Take out of oven
  6. Let it sit for a while
  7. Eat
