Pizza

Description

Indulge in the timeless simplicity and irresistible flavors of our classic pepperoni pizza. This iconic dish features a golden, crispy crust topped with tangy tomato sauce, gooey melted mozzarella cheese, and savory slices of pepperoni. With each bite, you'll experience the perfect harmony of textures and tastes, from the crunchy crust to the savory richness of the cheese and the zesty kick of the pepperoni. Whether enjoyed as a quick weeknight dinner or shared with friends during a casual gathering, this pepperoni pizza is sure to satisfy cravings and leave you craving just one more slice.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Pepperoni slices (lots)
  • Crushed red pepper flakes
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Roll the dough in a circle or square (your choice)
  3. Add sauce
  4. Add cheese
  5. Add pepperoni
  6. Garnish that sucker like salt bae
  7. Put in oven
  8. Wait a bit
  9. Take out of oven
  10. Let it sit for a while
  11. Eat
